Before provisioning volumes, every storage node needs an entry under
`nodes` in values.yaml declaring its storage pools, e.g.:
  nodes:
    <node-name>:
      pools:
        default:
          backend: lvmthin | zfs | loopfile
          device: /dev/disk/by-partlabel/r-miroir   # lvmthin
          # zfsDataset: pool/miroir                 # zfs
          # baseDir: /var/lib/miroir                # loopfile (reflink-capable FS)
StorageClasses select a pool with their `pool` knob (absent = "default").
